## Show Notes

<p>Very excited to share this podcast featuring a very special guest, Daniel Liebeskind. He is the co-founder and CEO of Topia, a platform for creative visual experiences that inspire authentic human connection.<br>
</p>
<p>From Wall Street to Silicon Valley, Daniel’s mission is to foster human connection through technology. He has been building offline communities his whole life and originally became an entrepreneur to build software that helped people manage their own personal community.<br>
</p>
<p>Daniel is a product creator and engineer, having coded nearly 30 applications over the last 6 years. He has built community platforms, contact managers, slack bots, real estate systems, trading platforms, knowledge ecosystems, and browser, VR and mobile games.<br>
<br>
Daniel believes that creativity is humanity's greatest gift and he has been on a quest to empower creators and make it easier for them to earn financial freedom by creating community and bringing people together to share in experiences.</p>
