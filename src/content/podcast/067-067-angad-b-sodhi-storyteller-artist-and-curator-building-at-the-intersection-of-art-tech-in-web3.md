## Show Notes

<p>Angad uses the power of art as a medium to break the taboo that mental health carries in India and globally. His art therapy sessions and workshops for corporates and blocked creative professionals increase creativity and communication skills. Shh... It&#39;s Taboo, his mental health event has enabled hundreds of people to share their mental health stories, empowering hundreds more to start talking about theirs. He co-founded The Colours of Change Foundation to spark conversations about locally relevant social issues in rural communities in India and to empower artists by the giving them a voice at the same time.</p>
