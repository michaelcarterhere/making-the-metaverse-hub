## Show Notes

<p>My guest today is Dennison Bertram, an avid futurist, artist and serial entrepreneur with a keen eye for the future of technology and its social impacts. He is an early block-chain technologist having founded the first Bitcoin Exchange for Czech Crowns (BuyBTC.cz), a technologically advanced physical bitcoin wallet (Aeternum Key-Vault), and a PayPal like digital currency wallet (Terrawallet). Dennison is the founder of DopeWars, DappHero, and PridePunks, the first CryptoPunks derivative. He also founded WithTally, which offers tools for decentralized decision making and governance for distributed ledger ecosystems.</p>
<p>Dennison is also a renowned fashion and commercial photographer that has shot for some of the world’s top names in Fashion and Commerce such as Nespresso, Heineken, Alitalia, Marie Claire, Elle, Versace, Gucci, Louis Vuitton and more. He has developed cutting edge 360 VR experiences and campaigns for brands such as MUDEC, Vogue Italia, I-D Magazine Online, Vice: Motherboard, The NorthFace and advertising agencies such as Publicis, Leo Burnett, McCann and more.</p>
