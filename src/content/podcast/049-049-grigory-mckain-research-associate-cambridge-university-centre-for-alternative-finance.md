## Show Notes

<p>Grigory is a research associate at the Cambridge Centre for Alternative Finance where he runs the Regulatory Knowledge Exchange project. Before joining the CCAF he worked at various tech companies, across functions but mainly on legal and research roles. He has also previously worked in the legal and research sectors in China and Central Asia and received an honours degree in law from the University of Edinburgh, specialising in international law. He teaches various courses on fintech and regulatory topics, including Schaffhausen Institute of Technology's course on Web 3.0.</p>
