## Show Notes

<p>Samuel Dahan is law professor at Queen’s University and is cross-appointed to Cornell Law School and Paris Dauphine Law School. A qualified lawyer and former EU official, his areas of interest include labour analytics and dispute resolution. He is the head of policy at the Deel Lab for Global Employment, a research consortium on labour policy and compliance analytics.</p>
<p>Dahan is the founder and Director of the&nbsp;<a href="https://conflictanalytics.queenslaw.ca/about"><strong>Conflict Analytics Lab</strong></a>, a global consortium concerned with the application of analytics to dispute resolution, and developed and launched <a href="https://myopencourt.org/news/"><strong>MyOpenCourt</strong></a>, an open-access legal technology. In addition, he is the recipient of the 2021 Stanley M. Corbett Award for Excellence in Teaching, and the 2020 INFORMS UPS Prize for best program in analytics.</p>
<p>At&nbsp;Constructor Learning&nbsp;(previously known as SIT Learning) Samuel is an instructor for the Masters of&nbsp;Management Analytics program.
