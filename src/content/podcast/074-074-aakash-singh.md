## Show Notes

<p>Aakash Singh is a Global People Management and Development Advisor. He is a mentor and a friend to over 37,000 students and 19 business owners globally. Aakash started his professional life as an engineer and eventually became a ‘talent architect’. Aakash helps people find their ‘perfect fit’, using the power of storytelling. He writes and speaks about HR, talent acquisition, and career development. </p>
<p>In this episode Aakash shares his insights on the role artificial intelligence is playing in HR. He covers everything from recruitment to resume writing. Aakash also offers some advice to students who are graduating and entering into the workforce. What are employers looking for? Should they be using ChatGPT? Listen to the episode for the answers! </p>
