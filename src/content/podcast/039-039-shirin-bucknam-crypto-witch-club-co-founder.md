## Show Notes

My guest today is Shirin Bucknam, co-founder of Crypto Witch Club, a trusted, equitable, and educational voice in the web3 space that is highly entertaining. Founded only a year ago, with co-founder Kaitlin McGowan, Crypto Witch Club has grown into a community of over 10,000 across channels and been featured in Marie Claire, Time, Bustle, GirlBoss, and more. Prior to this project, she launched POPcreative, a data-driven branding and marketing consultancy that executed campaigns for clients such as Kenneth Cole and FaceBook.
