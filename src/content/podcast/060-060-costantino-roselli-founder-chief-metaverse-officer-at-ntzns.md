## Show Notes

<p>My guest today brings incredible insight and a tremendous level of passion to web3. Costantino Roselli is Founder &amp; Chief Metaverse Officer at NTZNS and an Advisory Board Member on the Metaverse Fashion Council. He is a top brand storyteller, bestselling author, futurist and keynote speaker. He holds two masters in Digital Marketing and Leadership, a Diploma in Management of Arts, Culture &amp; Communication, a bachelor&#39;s in Computer Science, and over 21 certificates in Future Thinking, Gamification, Storytelling, Brand Management, Fashion &amp; Luxury Management, Communication, Growth Hacking Marketing, Human Psychology, and Human-Technology Interaction. Costantino is truly a leader in the space and it is a great honour to have him here today!</p>
