## Show Notes

My guest today is Liv Erickson, a true metaverse visionary. Liv jumped into the metaverse in 2014 and has not looked back. Since then Liv has worked for Microsoft, AWS and Mozilla where they are currently the team lead for Hubs. In their work at Mozilla they are leading the development of open, accessible and approachable virtual worlds. Liv is committed to making creator tools accessible to everyone so that online spaces are reflective of the visions of end users not just developers.
