## Show Notes

A small town girl who has taken the world and web3 by storm, Andrea Reade has too many skills and talents to list, and certainly cannot be placed in a box. Andrea, born a Leo, is a Canadian multidisciplinary artist that creates intuitive conceptualized artworks tailored to women empowerment, astrology and spirituality themes using AI & post photography mediums. Andrea has built an expansive community in web3, and has been featured in numerous exhibits including Art Basel, NFT NYC, and Futurist Toronto to name a few.
