## Show Notes

<p>Thrilled to have Arturo as my very first guest on the podcast we had an incredible conversation about NFT's, blockchain and the social side of the metaverse. Arturo has a lifetime of experience in PR. He has worked with dozens of global brands and most recently spent several years working on PR side of the first subway project in Lima, Peru.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Arturo is a professional communicator with 17 years of experience developing communication plans, managing communication crisis, creating, planning digital communication campaigns, and editing newsletters, blogs, press releases, articles, as well as developing media networking around the world. He is a curator of technology trends, fan of human knowledge and entrepreneurship values and innovation.</p>
<p>His next big drop is his 'My Other Skin' project that will make avatars and the offline humans that operate them stylish.&nbsp;</p>
