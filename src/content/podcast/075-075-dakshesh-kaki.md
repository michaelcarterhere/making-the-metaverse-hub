## Show Notes

<p>Dakshesh is a student at Middleton High School and is also studying computer science engineering at Hillsborough Community College. He works with artificial intelligence in business and in robotics. He has placed 6th in a national business competition with Future Business Leaders of America. He is the founder of a consulting firm. He is active in the robotics space, having competed in multiple First Robotics competitions and recently served as a student ambassador for the Orlando Regional 2024. In this episode we talk about AI in business, education, and in robotics.</p>
